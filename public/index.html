Support hub

Have a question?

Quick answers to the things people ask us most often.

How does an exchange work?

Enter the amount you want to send, pick your payment method, and confirm. We lock in the rate, you pay by card, and the crypto is settled to the destination wallet — usually within minutes.

How long does an exchange take?

Most orders complete in just a few minutes. Timing can vary with network congestion and payment confirmation, but the flow runs around the clock.

How do I buy BTC, ETH, or USDT with my card?

Use the exchange widget above: choose the crypto you want, enter the amount, select your card or local payment method, and follow the secure checkout to complete your purchase.

How can I reach support?

Email us any time at info@card2pay.app and our team will get back to you.
